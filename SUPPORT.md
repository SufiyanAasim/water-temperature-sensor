## Getting Help

1. Check the [Troubleshooting guide](docs/troubleshooting/Troubleshooting.md) first — it covers the most common hardware issues.
2. Search [existing issues](../../issues).
3. Open a new issue using the [bug template](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bug.yml) — include your board, library versions, and wiring.
